# Content Type Templates

*Source: https://blockcell.sqprod.co/sites/writers-block/docs/support/content-type-templates*

This section provides templates and structure guidelines for different types of support content across Block's business units. Each template is designed to maintain consistency while addressing the specific needs of different audiences and content types.

Use these templates to ensure your content follows established patterns and meets the expectations of both internal teams and external customers. The templates are organized by business unit and content type, with clear indicators of the intended audience.
