# Style Guides

Core writing principles and style guidelines that form the foundation of all Block support content.

## 📋 Overview

These guidelines ensure consistency, clarity, and quality across all Block support communications, regardless of business unit, audience, or channel.

## 📚 Style Guide Components

### [Writing Principles](./writing-principles.md)
**The 6 fundamental principles that guide all Block support content:**

1. **One article, one purpose** - Each piece addresses a single task or issue
2. **Be clear, concise, and purposeful** - Use plain language and active voice
3. **Structure content to guide and empower** - Organize logically with clear headings
4. **Write for the customer experience first** - Lead with customer intent and needs
5. **Design for a global audience** - Write for localization and accessibility
6. **Be consistent across channels** - Maintain unified voice and standards

### [Style Guidelines](./style-guidelines.md)
**Detailed style rules covering:**

- **Links** - How to hyperlink effectively and accessibly
- **Instruction Lists & Steps** - Writing clear, sequential instructions
- **Apologies** - When and how to use apology language appropriately
- **Support Team Terminology** - Consistent language for referring to teams and roles

### [Content Type Reference](./content-type-reference.md)
**Complete overview of available templates organized by:**

- Business unit (Square, Cash App)
- Content type (Articles, Quick Texts, Communications)
- Audience (External customers, Internal teams, Mixed)
- Template source links and usage guidelines

## 🎯 How to Use These Guidelines

### For Content Creators
1. **Start here** before writing any support content
2. **Reference frequently** during drafting and editing
3. **Use as validation** before publishing

### For Content Reviewers
1. **Check alignment** with core principles
2. **Validate style consistency** using detailed guidelines
3. **Ensure proper template usage** via content type reference

### For Team Leads
1. **Onboard new writers** with these fundamentals
2. **Establish team standards** based on these guidelines
3. **Maintain quality** through consistent application

## 🔗 Relationship to Templates

These style guides inform all templates in the [`../templates/`](../templates/) directory. Every template follows these principles and guidelines, adapted for specific:

- **Business units** (Square vs. Cash App requirements)
- **Content types** (Articles vs. Quick Texts vs. Communications)
- **Audiences** (Customer-facing vs. Internal team content)

## 📖 Additional Context

These guidelines are derived from Block's Writer's Block documentation and represent the current standards for support content across all Block business units. They build upon broader Block writing principles while addressing the specific needs of customer support communications.
