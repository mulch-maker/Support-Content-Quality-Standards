## 🎯 Square-Specific Guidelines

### **Voice & Tone**
Square content should be:
- **Professional yet approachable** - Business-focused but not intimidating
- **Solution-oriented** - Emphasize outcomes and business value
- **Empowering** - Help sellers feel confident and capable
- **Clear and direct** - Respect busy business owners' time

### **Audience Considerations**
- **Primary audience:** Small to medium business owners
- **Technical level:** Mixed - from tech-savvy to basic users
- **Context:** Often using Square during busy business operations
- **Goals:** Solve problems quickly, understand features, grow business

### **Content Structure**
All Square templates follow this hierarchy:
1. **Clear, action-oriented titles** - What the customer will accomplish
2. **Audience identification** - Who can complete the task
3. **Context setting** - What they need to know before starting
4. **Step-by-step instructions** - Clear, sequential guidance
5. **Outcome confirmation** - What success looks like
6. **Related resources** - Next steps and additional help

### **Formatting Standards**
- **Headings:** Sentence case, descriptive
- **Steps:** Numbered for sequential tasks, bulleted for options
- **UI elements:** Bold formatting for buttons, menus, field names
- **Links:** Descriptive text, not "click here" or raw URLs
- **Screenshots:** Consistent sizing, clear callouts when needed

## 🔧 Square-Specific Tools & Systems

### **Dashboard References**
- Always use "Square Dashboard" (not "dashboard" or "Square dashboard")
- Link to specific sections when possible
- Include full navigation path in first step
- Use exact UI text for buttons and menu items

### **Product Names**
Use official Square product names:
- Square Point of Sale (not "Square POS" in customer content)
- Square Dashboard
- Square for Restaurants
- Square Online
- Square Marketing

### **Permissions & Access**
Square has complex permission structures:
- Always specify who can complete tasks
- Link to permission management when relevant
- Consider different user roles (owner, team member, admin)
- Account for legacy vs. current subscription tiers
