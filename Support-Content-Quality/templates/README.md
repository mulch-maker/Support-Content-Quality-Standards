# Templates

Ready-to-use templates for creating consistent, high-quality Block support content across all business units and content types.

## 📋 Template Organization

Templates are organized by business unit to reflect how teams actually work, while maintaining consistency through shared [style guidelines](../style-guides/).

### [`square/`](./square/) - Square Support Content
Templates for Square's customer support and internal knowledge sharing.

### [`cash-app/`](./cash-app/) - Cash App Support Content  
Templates for Cash App's customer support, advocate resources, and internal communications.

## 🎯 Content Types by Audience

## 🚀 How to Use Templates

### 1. **Choose the Right Template**
- Identify your **business unit** (Square or Cash App)
- Determine your **audience** (customers, internal teams, or mixed)
- Select the **content type** that matches your goal

### 2. **Follow the Template Structure**
- Each template includes detailed guidance on structure, tone, and formatting
- Copy the template sections that apply to your content
- Customize examples and placeholders for your specific use case

### 3. **Apply Style Guidelines**
- All templates follow the [style guidelines](../style-guides/)
- Reference specific sections for detailed formatting rules
- Validate against the [writing principles](../style-guides/writing-principles.md)

### 4. **Review and Publish**
- Use the checklists in [`../tools/`](../tools/) for quality assurance
- Get appropriate approvals per your team's workflow
- Publish through your business unit's standard channels

## 🔄 Template Maintenance

### When to Update Templates
- Product features or flows change
- Style guidelines are updated
- User feedback indicates confusion
- New tools or systems are introduced

### How to Propose Changes
1. Test proposed changes with real content
2. Ensure alignment with [style guidelines](../style-guides/)
3. Document the rationale for changes
4. Update related examples and documentation

## 💡 Best Practices

### **Before You Start**
- Review the [writing principles](../style-guides/writing-principles.md)
- Understand your specific audience and their needs
- Gather all necessary information and resources

### **While Writing**
- Follow the template structure closely
- Use the exact formatting and style specified
- Include all required sections and components
- Test any links or interactive elements

### **Before Publishing**
- Validate against style guidelines
- Check for consistency with existing content
- Get appropriate team reviews and approvals
- Test content with target audience if possible
