# Cash App Templates

Templates specifically designed for Cash App support content, covering customer-facing help, advocate resources, and internal communications.

## 📋 Available Templates

### [Knowledge Articles](./knowledge-articles.md)
**Purpose:** Comprehensive advocate resources in CF1 (Salesforce Lightning)  
**Audience:** Internal - Cash App support advocates and specialists  
**Channel:** CF1 Knowledge base  
**Length:** 4,000 words (goal), 20,000 words (max)

**Use for:**
- Complex support procedures
- Policy explanations and guidance
- Multi-step troubleshooting workflows
- Regulatory and compliance procedures

### [Quick Texts](./quick-texts.md)
**Purpose:** Standardized response templates for customer interactions  
**Audience:** Mixed - Created by advocates, sent to customers  
**Channel:** All support channels (Voice, Messaging, Email)  
**Length:** 2-3 sentences, 100 words max

**Use for:**
- Common customer responses
- Policy explanations
- Status updates and confirmations
- Action guidance with embedded links

### [Help Center Articles](./help-center-articles.md)
**Purpose:** Self-service customer help on Cash App web properties  
**Audience:** External - Cash App customers (logged out)  
**Channel:** Cash App Help Center (web)  
**Length:** 1,500-4,500 characters

**Use for:**
- Feature explanations
- How-to instructions
- Educational content
- Issue identification and understanding

## 🎯 Cash App-Specific Guidelines

### **Voice & Tone**
Cash App content should be:
- **Warm and supportive** - Empathetic to customer financial concerns
- **Clear and accessible** - 6th-8th grade reading level
- **Solution-focused** - Emphasize what customers can do
- **Trustworthy** - Build confidence in financial transactions

### **Audience Considerations**
- **Primary audience:** Individual consumers, diverse demographics
- **Technical level:** Varies widely, assume basic smartphone literacy
- **Context:** Often dealing with financial stress or urgency
- **Goals:** Resolve issues quickly, understand features, feel secure

### **Channel-Specific Adaptations**

#### **Voice Support**
- Conversational, real-time interaction
- Can provide step-by-step guidance
- Advocate can complete actions for customer

#### **Messaging Support**  
- Concise, scannable text
- Action links for streamlined customer experience
- Asynchronous, customer-paced interaction

#### **Email Support**
- More formal tone
- Comprehensive information
- Detailed explanations and context

## 🔧 Cash App-Specific Tools & Features

### **Action Links (Messaging Only)**
- Use `{action: name=LinkName}` format
- Replace multi-step processes when possible
- Limit to 1-2 links per Quick Text
- Provide fallback instructions

### **Dynamic Fields**
- `{!Contact_FirstName}` for personalization
- `{!Case.SendSafely_URL_Type}` for secure file sharing
- Test all dynamic fields before publishing

### **Content Management Systems**
- **CF1 (Salesforce Lightning)** - Knowledge Articles
- **Contentful** - Quick Texts and Help Center
- **CCO Newsfeed** - Internal communications
