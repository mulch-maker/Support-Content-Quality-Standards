# Examples

Real-world examples of high-quality Block support content that demonstrate best practices and proper implementation of our templates and style guidelines.

## 📋 Purpose

These examples show how the templates and style guidelines translate into actual published content, helping content creators understand:

- **Practical application** of style guidelines
- **Successful implementation** of template structures
- **Effective tone and voice** for different audiences
- **Quality standards** for published content

## 📁 Organization

### [`square-examples/`](./square-examples/)

- How-to articles from Square Help Center
- Troubleshooting guides with real customer scenarios
- SquareWise posts showing internal best practices

### [`cash-app-examples/`](./cash-app-examples/)
Examples of Cash App support content across different channels:
- Knowledge articles from CF1

## 🎯 How to Use Examples

### **For Content Creation**
- Reference similar content types before starting new projects
- Adapt successful patterns and structures
- Learn from effective problem-solving approaches
- Understand audience-appropriate tone and complexity

### **For Content Review**
- Compare new content against proven examples
- Validate style consistency and quality standards
- Identify opportunities for improvement
- Ensure alignment with established best practices

### **For Training**
- Onboard new team members with concrete examples
- Demonstrate the difference between good and great content
- Show evolution of content quality over time
- Provide benchmarks for content creators

## 🔄 Example Maintenance

### **Selection Criteria**
Examples are chosen based on:
- **Performance metrics** - High customer satisfaction and usage
- **Style compliance** - Perfect adherence to guidelines
- **Effectiveness** - Successful problem resolution
- **Innovation** - Creative or particularly effective approaches

### **Regular Updates**
- **Quarterly review** of example relevance and accuracy
- **New additions** as exceptional content is identified
- **Removal** of outdated or superseded examples
- **Annotation updates** as guidelines evolve

## 💡 Contributing Examples

### **Nomination Process**
To suggest content for inclusion:
1. Identify content that exemplifies best practices
2. Verify performance metrics and customer feedback
3. Ensure compliance with current guidelines
4. Document what makes the example exceptional

### **Documentation Requirements**
Each example should include:
- **Context** - When and why the content was created
- **Performance data** - Usage metrics and customer feedback
- **Key features** - What makes it exemplary
- **Lessons learned** - Insights for future content creation
