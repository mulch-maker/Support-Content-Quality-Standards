## 🎯 What Makes Square Content Exceptional

### **Customer-Focused Structure**
- Clear, business-outcome oriented titles
- Immediate value proposition in opening
- Logical progression from simple to complex
- Actionable next steps and related resources

### **Business Context Awareness**
- Acknowledges time constraints of business owners
- Connects features to business value and growth
- Considers different business types and sizes
- Addresses common operational scenarios

### **Technical Clarity**
- Balances technical accuracy with accessibility
- Uses consistent Square product terminology
- Provides exact UI references and navigation paths
- Includes helpful visual aids when appropriate
