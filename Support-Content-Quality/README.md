# Support Content Quality

A comprehensive resource for Block's support content teams, providing style guidelines, templates, and best practices for creating consistent, high-quality customer and internal communications.

## 🎯 Purpose

This repository helps Block support teams create content that is:
- **Consistent** across all business units and channels
- **Clear** and accessible to all audiences
- **Compliant** with Block's writing standards
- **Efficient** to create and maintain

## 📁 Repository Structure

### [`style-guides/`](./style-guides/)
Core writing principles and style guidelines that apply to all Block support content.

- **[Writing Principles](./style-guides/writing-principles.md)** - The 6 fundamental principles
- **[Style Guidelines](./style-guides/style-guidelines.md)** - Detailed style rules and best practices
- **[Content Type Reference](./style-guides/content-type-reference.md)** - Overview of all available templates

### [`templates/`](./templates/)
Ready-to-use templates organized by business unit.

#### [`templates/square/`](./templates/square/)
Templates for Square support content:
- **[How-to Articles](./templates/square/how-to-articles.md)** - Customer-facing instructional content
- **[Troubleshooting Articles](./templates/square/troubleshooting-articles.md)** - Customer problem-solving guides  
- **[SquareWise Posts](./templates/square/squarewise-posts.md)** - Internal knowledge sharing

#### [`templates/cash-app/`](./templates/cash-app/)
Templates for Cash App support content:
- **[Knowledge Articles](./templates/cash-app/knowledge-articles.md)** - Internal advocate resources (CF1)
- **[Quick Texts](./templates/cash-app/quick-texts.md)** - Standardized response templates
- **[Help Center Articles](./templates/cash-app/help-center-articles.md)** - Customer web help content
- **[Newsfeed Communications](./templates/cash-app/newsfeed-communications.md)** - Internal team updates
- **[Step Writing Guide](./templates/cash-app/step-writing-guide.md)** - How to write clear instructions

### [`examples/`](./examples/)
Real-world examples of high-quality content following these guidelines.

### [`tools/`](./tools/)
Validation tools, checklists, and resources to ensure content quality.

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **New to Block support writing?** Start with [Writing Principles](./style-guides/writing-principles.md)
2. **Need a specific template?** Browse by business unit in [`templates/`](./templates/)
3. **Want to see examples?** Check out [`examples/`](./examples/)
4. **Ready to publish?** Use the checklists in [`tools/`](./tools/)
